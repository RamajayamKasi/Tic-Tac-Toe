Game Settings:

  1. Game Level: Select your preferred level to play the game.
  2. Team Player: If you have five players, you can activate or deactivite them.
  3. Background Color: Change the background color for the game.
  4. Board Themes: Select themes for the board.

  1. Board Show: The row and column will increase based on the selected level.
  2. Objective: Achieve the selected game level of your symbols in a row. For example, if you choose 5, aim to get 5 of your symbols in a row.
  3. Turns: Players take turns placing their symbols in an empty cell.
  4. Winning: Achieve the selected game level in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. For instance, if you select 5, aim for 5 symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  5. Draw: If the board is filled without a winner, it's a draw.
  6. Single Player : You can play against the computer opponent. Select your team and unselect any other team to play against the computer.
  7. Milti Player : You can play with your friend. Select your team and your friend's team.
